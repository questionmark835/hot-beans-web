Hot Beans Web - Job Board

Front-End Developer

We are looking for a talented front-end developer to join our team. You will work on creating beautiful and responsive websites for our clients.

Skills Required:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Experience with responsive design
  • Familiarity with modern front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Vue.js)
  • Good understanding of UX/UI principles

Back-End Developer

We are seeking a back-end developer to help build and maintain our web applications. You will work with databases and server-side logic.

Skills Required:

  • Experience with Node.js or PHP
  • Familiarity with relational and NoSQL databases
  • Experience with API development
  • Knowledge of server deployment and scaling

Full Stack Developer

We are looking for an experienced full stack developer to work across both the front-end and back-end of our web applications.

Skills Required:

  • Strong knowledge of both front-end and back-end technologies
  • Experience with React, Node.js, and databases
  • Ability to work independently and manage projects
  • Good problem-solving skills and debugging experience
